Allen Lee is a computer scientist and full stack software engineer building cyberinfrastructure for computational social science at Arizona State University since 2006. He led the initial development of the Digital Archaeological Record and currently co-directs the Network for Computational Modeling in the Social-Ecological Sciences, part of the NSF West Big Data Hub. He received his MSc from Indiana University - Bloomington in Computer Science / Informatics and his current research interests include collective action, complex adaptive systems, open science, and reproducibility insofar as it aids reuse and the overarching goals of science as a transparent, evidence-based and self-correcting institution.
