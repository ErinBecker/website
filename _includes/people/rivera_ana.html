Ana Lucrecia is a GIS Analyst and Instructor at Stanford Geospatial Center and San Jose State University. She holds a degree on Architecture, a M.Sc. in Environmental Systems and a M.A. in Geography. She uses Geo-Information Systems and Remote Sensing to research on areas related to urban planning, such as the impact of cities on the environment, formation of urban heat islands, vulnerability assessments of human settlements, flood risk analysis, public policies and urban poverty.
